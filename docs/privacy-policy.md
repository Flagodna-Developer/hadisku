# HadisKu Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: January 7, 2026_

HadisKu ("the App") is designed to be a Quran application that respects your privacy while providing enhanced features through trusted third-party services. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

## üì± Platform-Specific Features

**Important Notice**: HadisKu offers different features depending on your platform:

- **Mobile/Android Version**: Includes Google Sign-In and Google AdMob advertisements
- **Desktop Version**: Does not include Google Sign-In or advertisements - fully offline capable

The data collection described below applies primarily to the mobile/Android version. Desktop users experience minimal data collection limited to local storage only.

## üîç Data Collection

### Personal Information We Collect (Mobile/Android Only):

- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in with Google on mobile devices, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture to personalize your experience and sync your preferences across devices.
- **User Preferences**: Settings like font size, reading preferences, bookmarks, and notes are stored to enhance your experience.
- **Usage Analytics**: Basic app usage data to improve performance and user experience.
- **Advertising Data**: Google AdMob collects advertising identifiers and usage patterns to serve relevant ads.

### Desktop Version Data Collection:

- **User Preferences**: Settings, bookmarks, and notes stored locally only on your device
- **No Account Required**: No sign-in functionality; all data remains on your device
- **No Advertising Data**: No ads means no advertising-related data collection

### What We _Don't_ Collect (All Platforms):

- **Reading Content**: We do not track or analyze specific verses or content you read.
- **Personal Notes Content**: While we store your notes locally (and for sync on mobile), we do not analyze or access the content of your personal notes.
- **Sensitive Device Information**: Beyond basic app functionality requirements.

### Local Storage:

- **Mobile**: Stored locally on your device and securely in the cloud for sync purposes when signed in with Google.
- **Desktop**: Stored locally on your device only - no cloud sync available.

## üåê Network & Permissions

### Mobile/Android:

- **Internet Access**: Required for Google Sign-In, ad serving, syncing preferences across devices, and downloading additional content (audio recitations, translations, app updates).
- **Storage Permission**: Required to save user data, bookmarks, notes, and downloaded content.
- **Network State**: To determine connectivity for sync and download features.

### Desktop:

- **Internet Access**: Optional - only required for downloading additional content (audio recitations, translations, app updates).
- **Storage Permission**: Required to save user data, bookmarks, notes, and downloaded content locally.
- **Network State**: Not required for core functionality.

## üõ°Ô∏è Security

- **Data Protection**: All personal data (mobile only) is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only for technical support and app improvement purposes.
- **No Sale of Data**: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.
- **Desktop Privacy**: Desktop version keeps all data local with no transmission to external servers (except for optional content downloads).
